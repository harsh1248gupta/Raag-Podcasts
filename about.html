What is Podcast?

A podcast is a recording of audio discussion on a specific topic, like business or travel, that can be listened to. They're often found on iTunes and Spotify but are sometimes hosted on websites. This dynamic medium can be a perfect way to deliver your daily dose of inspiration wherever your audience might be.

How Profitable is Podcasts?

Most downloaded podcasts make over $100,000 a month. Earning a lot of money from the podcast, however, is not so easy and requires many conditions to be met, one of which is diversifying sources of income of your podcast. The fact is that most of podcasters do not earn much money from their podcasts.
